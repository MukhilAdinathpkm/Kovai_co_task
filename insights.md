# Public Transport Ridership Insights

### Key Findings from Daily Passenger Data (2019–2024)

-  **School Service Holidays:**
  - Sharp dips in *April, July, October,* and *December* mark school vacation periods.
  - <img width="989" height="590" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4802dc46-f9c1-45f5-aca3-ce5a2790fb26" />


-  **Busiest Months:**
  - *February* and *March* record the highest total ridership across all services.

-  **Busiest Days:**
  - *Tuesday* and *Wednesday* show the highest weekday passenger counts.

-  **Most Availed Service:**
  - *Rapid Route* remains the most frequently used mode every year.

-  **Least Availed Service:**
  - *Peak Service* consistently records the lowest ridership.

---

**Source:**  
Dataset — *Daily Public Transport Passenger Journeys by Service Type*  
Period — *2019–2024*  
Analysis — *Linear Regression, Seasonal Patterns, and EDA*
